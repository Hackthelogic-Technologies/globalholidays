Our Story

A Vision That Became a Journey

Global Holidays was founded in 2010 by our Managing Director, Mr. Rajaguru, whose passion for travel laid the foundation for what we are today. Although he began his professional career in academics—after completing his B.E. and M.E. degrees and serving as a Lecturer and Head of the Department (Computer Science)—his heart always gravitated toward tourism.


From a Dream to a Destination Expert

Driven by a strong interest in the travel industry, Mr. Rajaguru began by organizing Industrial Visits for engineering students, followed by tours for arts colleges and schools. What started as a small venture soon expanded into a trusted brand known for reliability and commitment. As Global Holidays grew, so did the range of services. We began handling:

  • Corporate tours
  • Family vacations
  • Honeymoon packages
  • Customized domestic and international trips


A Growing Team With a Growing Vision

Today, Global Holidays operates with a skilled and passionate team of 20+ dedicated professionals. Together, we have expanded our services beyond the borders of India, offering memorable travel experiences to top international destinations such as:

  • Thailand
  • Malaysia
  • Singapore
  • Dubai
  • and many more...
Every international tour is organized and managed directly by our team, ensuring seamless planning and superior service.
